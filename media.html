MARCH 2022 | WOMEN’S DAY CELEBRATION

Similar to IWD values, SBC Tanzania Ltd upholds women’s achievements, acknowledges their tenacity, recognizes challenges and focuses on gender equality and women’s rights. On this day and everyday, SBC celebrated and appreciated the contribution of women at SBC and worldwide.

MAY 2022 | UDSM STUDENTS’ PLANT VISIT

3rd year students Bachelor of Commerce in Marketing Management from University of Dar es Salaam visited the plant at SBC Tanzania , Kipawa Dar es Salaam to learn the production process and marketing strategies from our marketing experts.

MAY 2022 | FIRE TRAINING

The staff of SBC Tanzania Ltd receiving training on how to combat fire incidents from the Fire brigade and rescue.

JUNE 2022 | PETCO LAUNCH

SBC Tanzania Ltd is honored to be one of the 8 founders of PETCO Tanzania and to attend the launch ceremony graced by his Hon. Selemani Jafo, Minister of Union and Environment. PETCO is a company aimed at managing the collection and processing of plastic bottles after use.

OCTOBER 2022 | DESK PROJECT

Once again, we partnered with Dar Rotary Club to give 100 desks to Mkoani primary school, in Ilala district, Dar es Salaam. The exercise was accompanied with tree planting aimed at creating a conducive environment for the students.

OCTOBER 2022 | KITU ROHO INAPENDA, WHAT IS IT?

Imagine a busy day at work, everything is fine, but you are still feeling like something is missing. There’s a yearning in your heart that if you can get a cold Pepsi, everything will be fine. But you don’t do that because, it’s a busy day and you don’t have the time to stop at the local shop to grab one.

When the day ends, you decide to make a stop at the local shop, you grab a cold Pepsi from the fridge, just from touching the glass bottle you are already filling the missing piece, then you start gulping non-stop until you are satisfied.

Well, that is KITU ROHO INAPENDA! Something that your soul loves. It’s that one thing which quenches your thirst like nothing else.

NOVEMBER 2022 | EMPOWERING STUDENTS WITH DAR ROTARY CLUB

SBC Tanzania Ltd has been partnering with Dar Rotary Club since last 2 decades to help improve the livelihood of the people of Tanzania.

We share a joint responsibility across both organizations to serve the communities and provide support to empower the upcoming generation and the underprivileged people of this beautiful country.

We will cherish the memories created with Dar Rotary club at different Schools and Education Centers.
